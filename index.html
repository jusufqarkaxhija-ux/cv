Prof.Ass.Dr. Jusuf QARKAXHIJA

Dean- Faculty of Computer Science, AAB College

e-mail: jusuf.qarkaxhija@aab-edu.net

Prof. Ass. Dr. Jusuf Qarkaxhija was born on May 16, 1967, in Gjakova, where he also completed his primary and secondary education. In 1992, he graduated from the Faculty of Electrical Engineering at the University of Prishtina, majoring in Electronics, and earned the title Graduate Engineer of Electronics. This title, obtained at that time with 300 ECTS credits, is today equivalent to a Master of Science in Electronics. In addition, he completed a second scientific master's degree in Management and Informatics, earning the title Master of Science in Management and Informatics. Furthermore, in October 2015, he defended his doctoral dissertation at the European University of Tirana, titled "Broadband Internet and E-Commerce: The Case of Kosovo". The dissertation falls within the field of Informatics and is available at the following link: https://uet.edu.al/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/Jusuf_Qarkaxhia.pdf. The degree obtained is in the profile of Information Systems.

His professional experience began in the pre-war period with training courses in Help Desk and Fortran 4. After 1999, he continued attending various training courses. Since 2005, he has served as an Information Technology instructor at the vocational training school "Gjon Nikollë Kazazi" in Gjakova. For a three-year period, he also collaborated with the companies Expik from Prishtina and Ati-Kos from Gjakova as an ECDL (European Computer Driving Licence) examiner. For one year (2010–2011), he held the position of lecturer of Mathematics and Business Statistics at the Faculty of Economics, University of Prizren. In the winter semester of the 2011/2012 academic year, he was employed at AAB College – Gjakova branch. On October 1, 2012, he was appointed Coordinator of the Faculty of Economics at AAB College – Gjakova branch (2012–2014). From 2014 until October 1, 2017, he served as Director of the AAB College branch in Gjakova as well as lecturer of computer science courses at the Faculty of Computer Science in Prishtina.

Since October 1, 2017, he has been serving as the Dean of the Faculty of Computer Science, during which period he was also awarded the academic title of Assistant Professor (Prof. Ass. Dr.).
